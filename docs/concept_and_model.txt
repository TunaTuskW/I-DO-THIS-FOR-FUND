MACRO ANALYST AGENT - CONCEPT AND MODEL
=======================================

1. WHAT IT IS
This is a 24/7 autonomous quantitative agent that monitors global markets, processes data through deep learning classifiers, and generates institutional-grade macro reports. It runs completely automatically in the background using your computer's scheduler. Its presentation layer uses an ultra-minimal "Brutalist Terminal" text-block layout to ensure a data-dense, cross-platform reading experience designed for instant mobile parsing (e.g., Discord/Telegram).

2. THE DUAL ARCHITECTURE: PYTHON (.py) vs MARKDOWN (.md)
To achieve zero hallucinations, the agent's brain is split into two halves:

THE QUANTITATIVE ENGINE (The .py Files)
The Python scripts (e.g., fetch_market_data.py, build_report.py) act as the cold, emotionless math engine. 
- They pull raw real-time data from Yahoo Finance, FRED, and the ECB.
- They calculate complex metrics like the Macro Condition Score (MCS), Shannon Entropy, Brier Scores, and Fractional Kelly Sizing.
- They run the Deep Learning models (HMM and MLP) to determine the market regime.
- They deterministically assemble the raw numbers into the final "Brutalist UI" formatting.

THE REASONING ENGINE (The .md Setup File)
The markdown file (macro_agent_setup.md) is the strict "Instruction Manual" for the Large Language Model (the AI). 
- It tells the AI exactly how to read the JSON output produced by the Python scripts.
- It forces the AI through the 5-step Structured Analytical Protocol (SAP) to verify the math, look for contradictions, and identify a single root cause.
- It governs the "AI Strategic Assumptions Layer" so the AI doesn't guess or speculate wildly.

3. THE END-TO-END WORKFLOW
- Data Collection: Every cycle, the python scripts download real-time market data across global equities (NDX, DAX, FTSE, N225, HSI, SHANGHAI, KOSPI, TASI, DFM), global fiat FX pairs, commodities (Gold, Silver, Copper), and institutional crypto ETF/spot flows.
- Mathematical Scoring: The python scripts score the market stress and momentum.
- AI Logic Guardrails (SAP): Before the AI is allowed to write the narrative, the `.md` prompt forces it through the strict 5-step SAP logic test.
- Report Generation & Delivery: The agent writes a clean, professional narrative. The final Brutalist-formatted report is instantly pushed to your Discord channel.
- Cryptographic Proof: Every report is digitally signed and "hash-chained" (TruChain) so that its history cannot be secretly altered later.

4. THE MATH & MODELS BEHIND THE SCENES
Instead of relying purely on an AI's text generation, the agent uses hard quantitative models to determine the true state of the market:

- Macro Condition Score (MCS): A composite number measuring overall market stress based on stock momentum, interest rates, energy prices, and cross-asset behavior.
- Hidden Markov Models (HMM) & Kalman Filters: These algorithms smooth out daily noise to figure out the "true" underlying regime (Risk-On, Risk-Off, or Transitional).
- Deep Classifier (MLP): A trained neural network that double-checks the HMM. 
- GARCH Volatility: Predicts upcoming volatility spikes before they happen.
- Institutional Footprinting: Natively supports advanced institutional footprinting, including a 20-day rolling Volume Heat and Market Extremes diagnostic tracker.
- Synthetic Corporate Credit: Mathematically estimates high-yield credit stress even when traditional data is delayed.

5. ADVANCED MODEL GOVERNANCE & TACTICAL ASSUMPTIONS
The agent uses advanced self-monitoring techniques to ensure it does not suffer from AI over-confidence:
- KL Divergence & Structural Ambiguity Index (SAI): The agent mathematically measures the disagreement between its HMM and MLP models. High SAI triggers an automatic confidence downgrade.
- Rolling Brier Score: A self-calibration tool that tracks if the agent's historical probability forecasts have been accurate.
- 1.5 Std Dev Dynamic Boundaries: Continuously tracks volatility thresholds to automatically invalidate outdated narratives upon statistical sweeps.
- Epistemic Sizing & Fractional Kelly: The system calculates pure Shannon Entropy to measure statistical chaos. Instead of a binary "trade/no-trade" decision, the agent uses a modified Fractional Kelly Criterion—penalized by its Brier score—to scale exposure based on its exact mathematical edge.
- Statistical Noise Filtering: If the primary Kalman classifier drops below 60.0% probability, the system overrides the output with explicit "NO PREDICT / No Comment" tags to prevent trading on statistical coin flips.

6. ESCALATION SYSTEM
The agent respects your time and won't spam you with panicky alerts. It only flags urgency when the math demands it:
- ROUTINE: Normal market updates.
- ELEVATED: Moderate volatility spikes, sudden currency squeezes, or early-warning transition signals.
- CRITICAL: Extreme mathematical stress (e.g., VIX > 30, simultaneous stock and bond crashes). Discord will ping you heavily for these.

7. THE AUTOMATED SCHEDULE
The agent manages three distinct workflows on its own:
- The 4-Hour Briefing: A quick pulse-check of the active market session.
- The Daily Roll: At midnight, it pushes a master document containing the last 72 hours of updates.
- The Sunday Synthesis: A deep-dive research report summarizing the entire week and previewing the week ahead.

8. OFFLINE MODEL TRAINING & BACKTESTING
The deep learning models (HMM and MLP) are not static. The agent includes an offline training pipeline (train_models.py) that allows you to periodically re-train the neural networks on fresh market data. Whenever you train the models, it automatically generates a Backtest Report (backtest_results.md) so you can empirically verify the agent's mathematical edge before it trades or analyzes live markets.

By anchoring the AI to a rigid mathematical backbone, the agent provides institutional-quality insights with deterministic reliability.
