MACRO ANALYST AGENT - CONCEPT AND MODEL
=======================================

1. WHAT IT IS
This is a 24/7 autonomous agent that monitors global markets, analyzes data, and writes institutional-grade macro reports. It runs completely automatically in the background using your computer's scheduler, without you needing to do anything.

2. THE END-TO-END WORKFLOW
- Data Collection: Every cycle, the agent automatically downloads real-time market data from Yahoo Finance, FRED, and the ECB. It tracks stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies, and even crypto ETF flows.
- Mathematical Scoring: It runs this data through advanced python scripts to mathematically score how stressed the market is.
- AI Logic Guardrails (SAP): Before the AI is allowed to write a report, it is forced through a strict 5-step logic test (The Structured Analytical Protocol). It must verify the math, look for contradictions, and identify a single root cause. This prevents the AI from "guessing" or hallucinating.
- Report Generation: The agent writes a clean, professional narrative explaining what happened, why it happened, and what the risks are going forward.
- Discord Delivery: The final report is instantly pushed to your Discord channel.
- Cryptographic Proof: Every report is digitally signed and "hash-chained" (TruChain) so that its history cannot be secretly altered later.

3. THE MATH & MODELS BEHIND THE SCENES
Instead of relying purely on an AI's text generation, the agent uses hard quantitative models to determine the true state of the market:

- Macro Condition Score (MCS): A composite number measuring overall market stress based on stock momentum, interest rates, energy prices, and cross-asset behavior (like Gold vs. Silver, or safe-havens vs. risk-assets).
- Hidden Markov Models (HMM) & Kalman Filters: These algorithms smooth out daily noise to figure out the "true" underlying regime (Risk-On, Risk-Off, or Transitional).
- Deep Classifier (MLP): A trained neural network that double-checks the HMM. If the two models disagree, the agent automatically flags an anomaly.
- GARCH Volatility: Predicts upcoming volatility spikes before they happen.
- Synthetic Corporate Credit: Mathematically estimates high-yield credit stress even when traditional data is delayed.

4. ESCALATION SYSTEM
The agent respects your time and won't spam you with panicky alerts. It only flags urgency when the math demands it:
- ROUTINE: Normal market updates.
- ELEVATED: Moderate volatility spikes, sudden currency squeezes, or early-warning transition signals.
- CRITICAL: Extreme mathematical stress (e.g., VIX > 30, simultaneous stock and bond crashes). Discord will ping you heavily for these.

5. THE AUTOMATED SCHEDULE
The agent manages three distinct workflows on its own:
- The 4-Hour Briefing: A quick pulse-check of the active market session.
- The Daily Roll: At midnight, it pushes a master document containing the last 72 hours of updates.
- The Sunday Synthesis: A deep-dive research report summarizing the entire week and previewing the week ahead.

6. OFFLINE MODEL TRAINING & BACKTESTING
The deep learning models (HMM and MLP) are not static. The agent includes an offline training pipeline (train_models.py) that allows you to periodically re-train the neural networks on fresh market data. Whenever you train the models, it automatically generates a Backtest Report (backtest_results.md) so you can empirically verify the agent's mathematical edge before it trades or analyzes live markets.
7. ADVANCED MODEL GOVERNANCE & TACTICAL ASSUMPTIONS
The agent uses advanced self-monitoring techniques to ensure it does not suffer from AI over-confidence:
- KL Divergence & Structural Ambiguity Index (SAI): The agent mathematically measures the disagreement between its HMM and MLP models. If the disagreement (SAI) gets too high, it automatically degrades its confidence level and flags a model divergence.
- Rolling Brier Score: A self-calibration tool that tracks if the agent's historical probability forecasts have actually been accurate over time.
- 1.5 Std Dev Dynamic Boundaries: Continuously tracks volatility thresholds to automatically invalidate outdated narratives the moment a statistical sweep occurs.
- AI Strategic Assumptions Layer: At the end of every update, the AI must explicitly document its market interpretation, tactical lean, confidence level, and exact invalidation conditions in a non-theatrical format.
8. STATISTICAL NOISE FILTERING & "NO PREDICT" GOVERNANCE
The deterministic synthesis engine is trained to explicitly filter out market noise. If the primary Kalman classifier drops below 60.0% probability (indicating a statistical coin flip), the system refuses to force a directional lean. It overrides the output with explicit "NO PREDICT / No Comment" tags, clearly advising the user to stand aside rather than trading on noise.

By anchoring the AI to a rigid mathematical backbone, the agent provides institutional-quality insights with deterministic reliability.
